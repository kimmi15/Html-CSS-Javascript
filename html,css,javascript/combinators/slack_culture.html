Working and thriving

We want people to thrive at slack; we believe you do your best work when you feel best. Here are a just a few benefits we're proud to provide our team with

Learning & development

Recieve an annual allowance for professional development, plus a monthly bonus you can use towards anything. That means anything: a gym membership, your pet sitter, a new work look. Whatever matters most to you.

Health & wellness

Free medical, dental care and eye care for you and your family - Slack pays 100% of your pemiums.

Parental leave

A lot of us have families, of all sorts of shapes and sizes, and we welcome each new addition with generous parental leave and "bonding leave".

Happier weekdays

A culture light on meetings (or that's our aim, at least) with guest speakers and opportunities to get to know your team better through events like weekle catered lunches and breakfasts, Thursday Socials and game nights. But we don't stay in the office 24/7.
